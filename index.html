About Salgaa

Salgaa Area Program is located in Rongai Sub County, Nakuru County and covers 5 sub locatons in Viso and Mosop Wards. The sub

Locatons are Gchea, Boito, Chepseon, Umoja and Mwimaita. Salgaa town is a fast growing industrial Town with a population of 19,954

male, 20,098 female and 44,668 children
